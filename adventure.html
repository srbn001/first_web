Things to Do in Sikkim

Nestled in the Himalayas, Sikkim is a paradise for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and spiritual pilgrims. With its breathtaking landscapes, rich Buddhist culture, and pristine environment, Sikkim offers a unique blend of experiences. From the majestic Kanchenjunga to ancient monasteries, from thrilling treks to serene lakes, Sikkim is a destination that captivates every traveler's heart.

Explore Diverse Experiences

Trekking & Hiking

Explore the Himalayan trails with breathtaking views of Kanchenjunga and lush valleys. Popular treks include Goecha La, Dzongri, and Green Lake.

Monasteries & Pilgrimages

Visit ancient monasteries like Rumtek, Pemayangtse, and Tashiding to experience spiritual serenity and Buddhist culture.

Lakes & Waterfalls

Discover pristine lakes like Tsomgo, Khecheopalri, and Gurudongmar, along with stunning waterfalls throughout the state.

Hill Stations

Relax in beautiful hill stations like Gangtok, Pelling, and Ravangla offering panoramic views and pleasant climates.

Wildlife Encounters

Explore rich biodiversity in national parks like Khangchendzonga, home to red pandas, snow leopards, and diverse bird species.

Village Tourism

Experience authentic Sikkimese culture by staying in traditional villages and learning about local customs and lifestyles.

Top Tourist Destinations

Gangtok

The capital city with stunning views of Kanchenjunga

Tsomgo Lake

Sacred glacial lake at 12,400 feet altitude

Nathula Pass

Historic mountain pass on the Indo-China border
