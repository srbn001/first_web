About Sikkim

Sikkim is a state in northeastern India known for its biodiversity, alpine and subtropical climates, and stunning views of Kangchenjunga, the highest peak in India and third highest on Earth.


Capital: Gangtok

Area: 7,096 km²

Population: 610,577


Sikkim is a popular tourist destination for its Buddhist monasteries, trekking routes, and pristine natural beauty.

Travel Information

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Sikkim is between March and May, and from September to November. These months offer pleasant weather and clear views of the Himalayas.

Permits

Foreign nationals require an Inner Line Permit (ILP) to visit Sikkim. Indian nationals need a Protected Area Permit (PAP) for certain restricted areas.

Popular Destinations

Gangtok, Nathula Pass, Tsomgo Lake, Pelling, Yuksom, Lachung, and Lachen are among the most popular destinations in Sikkim.
